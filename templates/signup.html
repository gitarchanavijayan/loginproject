Information

Education refers to the discipline that is concerned with methods of teaching and learning in schools or school-like environments,

as opposed to various nonformal and informal means of socialization.

These aims may include the development of understanding, rationality, kindness, and honesty.

Various researchers emphasize the role of critical thinking in order to distinguish education from indoctrination.

Today, educational goals increasingly encompass

new ideas such as the liberation of learners, skills needed for modern society, empathy, and complex vocational skills.
